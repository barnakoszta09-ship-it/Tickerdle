About Tickerdle

Tickerdle is a free daily stock market word game inspired by Wordle. Every day, a new S&P 500 ticker symbol is selected — your job is to guess it in 6 tries or fewer using color-coded clues.

How to Play

Type any valid S&P 500 ticker symbol and press Enter. After each guess, the tiles reveal how close you are:

Green — correct letter, correct position.
Yellow — correct letter, wrong position.
Gray — letter not in the ticker at all.

All tickers come from the S&P 500 — the index of the 500 largest publicly traded companies in the United States, including names like AAPL, MSFT, AMZN, and GOOGL.

Game Modes

Daily — One new ticker puzzle per day, shared by all players worldwide. Compare your results with friends using the share feature.

Endless — Unlimited puzzles. Keep guessing new tickers as long as you want, and build your streak.

Higher / Lower — Compare two S&P 500 companies and guess which has the larger market capitalization. A finance twist on the classic game.

Who is it for?

Tickerdle is built for investors, traders, finance students, and anyone who wants a fun daily brain workout with a stock market twist. Whether you're a seasoned Wall Street professional or just starting to learn about the stock market, Tickerdle is a great way to test and sharpen your knowledge of S&P 500 companies.

Is it free?

Yes — completely free. No sign-up required, no subscription, no ads. If you enjoy playing, you can support the developer with a coffee.
