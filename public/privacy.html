Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

Tickerdle ("we", "our", or "the game") is a free daily word game available at tickerdle.org and on Android. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how optional rewarded advertising works.

What we collect

  • Player UUID — a randomly generated identifier stored in your browser's localStorage. Created on your first visit and never linked to your real identity.
  • Player name — the display name you choose when you first play. Stored in localStorage and submitted to the leaderboard.
  • Game scores — your leaderboard scores (streak counts) associated with your player UUID and chosen name.
  • Bug reports — if you submit a bug report, we store the description, game mode, your browser's user-agent string, and your IP address. Used only to diagnose issues.

Rewarded Advertising

Tickerdle is free to play. To keep it that way, certain optional in-game hints (such as the "Reveal a Letter" hint on your final guess) require watching a short rewarded video ad. Watching the ad is entirely voluntary — you can always skip the hint and play without it.

When you choose to watch a rewarded ad, a third-party advertising SDK may collect and process data to deliver and measure the ad. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID on Android, or a cookie-based identifier on web)
  • IP address (used to determine approximate region for ad targeting)
  • Browser or device type and operating system version
  • Ad interaction data (whether the ad was watched, skipped, or completed)

This data is collected and processed by the ad network, not by Tickerdle directly. We do not receive or store any personally identifiable information from ad interactions.

Ad networks we use or plan to use:

  • Google AdSense / AdMob — subject to Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalised ads at adssettings.google.com.

You are never required to watch an ad. All core gameplay — daily puzzles, endless mode, higher/lower mode, and leaderboards — is completely free. Rewarded ads are strictly optional and only appear when you choose to unlock a bonus hint.

What we do NOT collect

  • No real names, email addresses, or passwords
  • No precise location data
  • No payment information
  • No tracking cookies placed by Tickerdle itself
  • No data sold or rented to third parties

How we use your data

  • Your UUID and name are used solely to display your score on the leaderboard
  • Bug reports are used only to fix issues and are not shared with anyone
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any of your data with third parties (ad networks handle their own data independently under their own policies)

Data storage

Game state, your name, and UUID are stored locally in your browser's localStorage. Server-side leaderboard data is stored on a private VPS and is not shared with any third party.

Your choices

  • Opt out of personalised ads — visit adssettings.google.com or use your device's advertising settings (Settings → Privacy → Advertising on Android).
  • Skip rewarded hints — you can always decline to watch an ad. The hint is optional; no core gameplay feature is locked behind an ad.
  • Delete your local data — clear your browser's localStorage or app data to remove your UUID, name, and saved game state.

Children

Tickerdle is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Rewarded ads are also not shown to users identified as under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the game after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
