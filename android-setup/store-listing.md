## Short description (80 chars max)
Guess the S&P 500 stock ticker. A new puzzle every day.

## Full description (4000 chars max)

Can you identify S&P 500 companies by their stock ticker symbol?

Tickerdle is a daily word-game for market enthusiasts. Each day a new ticker is chosen from the S&P 500 — guess it in up to 6 tries. After every guess you get color-coded feedback:

🟩 Green — right letter, right position
🟨 Yellow — right letter, wrong position
⬛ Grey — letter not in the ticker

**Daily Mode**
One puzzle per day, shared with every player worldwide. Build your daily streak — come back every day to keep it alive.

**Endless Mode**
Keep guessing. Each correct answer starts a new round with a fresh ticker. How long can you chain?

**Higher or Lower**
Two companies appear side by side. Which one has the bigger market cap? Tap higher or lower and see how many you can get in a row.

**Leaderboard**
Compete against other players. Daily streak leaders, endless streak champions, and H/L high scores — all tracked globally.

No accounts. No subscriptions. Optional rewarded ads unlock bonus hints — watching is always voluntary. Just the market, a keyboard, and your knowledge.
